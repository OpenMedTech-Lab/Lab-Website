I am a Consultant Physiotherapist and Researcher at the Centre for the Rehabilitation of the Paralysed (CRP) in Savar, Dhaka, Bangladesh. My work focuses on neurological recovery and functional outcomes, with research interests in assistive technology, neurostimulation, community reintegration, treatment effectiveness, accessibility, employment, and disability policy. 

Through my clinical practice and research, I strive to advance physiotherapy, foster inclusive rehabilitation services, and improve the lives of people with disabilities in Bangladesh.
