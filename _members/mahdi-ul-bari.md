I am a Clinical Physiotherapist at the Centre for the Rehabilitation of the Paralysed (CRP) in Savar, Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I provide physiotherapy care, develop treatment programmes, and collaborate with interdisciplinary healthcare teams to support patient recovery and safety. My clinical training includes placements across orthopaedics, neurology, spinal cord injury rehabilitation, cardiopulmonary care, and paediatrics, providing me with broad experience in evidence-based rehabilitation practice. My research interests focus on neurological and musculoskeletal rehabilitation, particularly stroke rehabilitation and spinal cord injury.

Through my clinical practice, research, and educational activities, I am committed to advancing rehabilitation science and improving outcomes for individuals living with disabilities.
